The Score Boar is an Internet of Things project that allows the students and members of Galvanize (Platte) to check if the highly sought after ping-pong table is available before having to go to the basement to physically check.
Click "Current Game" to see what the current score is on the table at this moment in time. (If it's 0-0 the table is available for use!)
